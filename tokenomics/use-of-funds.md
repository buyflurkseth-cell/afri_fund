# Use of Funds

Once the **$2M community soft cap** is met, the Ugandan government will contribute
**$10M**, forming a **$12M Development Fund**. These funds are allocated entirely
to tangible infrastructure — solar energy farms, road construction, and water
supply systems. All project expenditures are publicly reported and subject to
community oversight.
