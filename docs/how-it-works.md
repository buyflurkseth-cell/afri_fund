# How AfriFUND Works

1. **Raise** — investors buy AFR during the presale (Stages 1–6) on Solana.
2. **Build** — the $12M Development Fund finances solar energy, roads and water.
3. **Earn** — 100% of project profits are distributed **quarterly** to holders,
   pro-rata to their AFR balance (estimated 15–25% annual yield).
4. **Grow** — ambassadors expand the community and earn unilevel commissions
   (20% / 5% / 3% / 2% / 1%) plus a share of the 500M AFR bonus pool.

See [Tokenomics](../tokenomics/README.md) and the
[Ambassador Program](../ambassador/README.md) for full details.
