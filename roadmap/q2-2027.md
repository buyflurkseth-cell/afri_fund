# Q2 2027 & Beyond — Decentralization

**Status: ⏳ Upcoming**

AfriFUND transitions to a fully decentralized DAO. An on-chain investment platform
enables trustless project selection and automatic dividend distribution. The model
expands to West Africa and beyond, creating a continuous, community-governed
investment network for the continent.

* ⏳ Transition to Fully Decentralized DAO
* ⏳ On-Chain Investment Platform
* ⏳ Trustless Project Selection
* ⏳ Automatic Dividend Distribution
* ⏳ Expansion to West Africa & Beyond
