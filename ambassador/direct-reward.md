# Direct Reward (20%)

Earn **20%** on every direct purchase made through your referral link. The minimum
purchase is 0.033 SOL (~$5): an investor who sends 0.033 SOL receives 1,320 AFR,
and you instantly receive 264 AFR. This example uses the Stage 5 rate of
1 SOL = 40,000 AFR.
