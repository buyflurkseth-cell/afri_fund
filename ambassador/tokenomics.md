# Tokenomics Overview

High-level breakdown of the **10,000,000,000 AFR** supply, highlighting the
allocations most relevant to ambassadors. All figures are fixed on-chain.

| Pool | Amount | Relevance to ambassadors |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Referral Reward Pool** | 1,500,000,000 AFR | Funds your unilevel commissions (Levels 1–5+) |
| **Ambassador Bonus Pool** | 500,000,000 AFR | Shared among ambassadors with 500+ active team |

Together these two pools reserve **2,000,000,000 AFR (20% of supply)** to reward
community growth.
