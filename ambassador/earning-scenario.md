# Earning Scenario

A realistic projection: if you bring **500 direct referrals** and each of them
brings just two more (who in turn bring two more), your commissions across
Levels 1–4 combine with your Bonus Pool share to a potential total of over
**4,500,000 AFR** — which at DEX listing (1 SOL = 6,000 AFR) equates to roughly
**750+ SOL**.
